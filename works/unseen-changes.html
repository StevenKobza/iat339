Unseen Changes Process Analysis

This project is my most recent creation. It was created during my Moving Images course during the Fall 2020 semester. I found myself working on it alone, and as such I needed to develop a couple of very important skills that are used whenever creating your own film.

I found myself reaching into the bag of tricks that was created whenever I took part in Digital Image Design a couple of years ago, I remembered how to create a camera that moves across the screen. And I used that to create the panning effect that is present in my film.


 

I also found myself needing to work on iterating my project versus just restarting multiple times over and over again. As such here is one of my earlier drafts before I settled on some things.



From there, I started to finalize more and more of the project and ended up with this as the final product of that project.
