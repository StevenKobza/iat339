In these slides, you will see two of the photos that we had included on the wall in the intro to the film. Each one of those shots was taken by us to be included in the film. They were all taken in one day but they had to convey time passing so we had the actresses change clothes throughout the different shots.

The other sets of images are the behind the scenes images, they're ones that show how we had things set up and how things looked before we ended up filming and colour correcting them. They also show how much fun we had while creating this project.


During this project, I learned that not everything ends up going the way that you want them to and you need to be able to adjust your goals and your schedule. We had multiple delays in filming and these things were accounted for by us and we were able to work around them. We ended up with a film that I'm very proud of.
